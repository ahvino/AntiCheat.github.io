With the ever-increasing popularity of video games, there has also been a dramatic increase in cheats and cheat software. At present some of the development companies behind the making of the cheat software are making millions of dollars annually. According to a Vice article, one of the most popular cheat developer organizations, “raked in $77 million from selling cheats”. There has obviously been a significant incentive for cheat developers to continually develop these cheats, but they cause significant harm to a game’s player base. There currently is a wide variety of anti-cheat software but so far, it’s been a losing battle. Already, these cheat developers are using AI technology in their software to make things less hospitable for players who play fairly, luckily, AI is now also being leveraged to detect cheats.
