The Problem

A constant problem for developers who try to counter cheat developers is that it’s not a problem that is resolved from a single deployment of updates. Cheat software is constantly being updated to adapt to the current game and so very quickly, a game’s anti-cheat software is exploited by cheat developers and new ways of exploiting are found and used in new cheats. AI software is currently showing promise in helping to mitigate this issue.

AI to the Rescue

One of the companies combating cheat developers is SARD Anti-Cheat. SARD is a anti cheat that empowers the developers to ensure the integrity required for fair play within a game. Though controversial, SARD leverages kernel-based tools on top of their AI and ML modules to provide future proof game security. This means that an anti-cheat may still be viable regardless of updates to the cheat software. If the anti-cheat software is able to adapt and improve itself, the cheat developer’s product becomes significantly less reliable. Again, the fact that this is kernel based does cause some concern. Kernel level anti cheat provides full access to all the hardware and software on your machine. It’s not unreasonable to think that this could lead to potentially serious issues. If the kernel-level anti cheat is affected in some way, this could leave all users who use cheats and also those who don’t vulnerable. It’s very possible that you may have caused a more serious issue with the addition of any type of kernel based anti-cheat. Having said all this, SARD still might be a necessary evil for some developers who may not be able to have a game without a more robust anti-cheat. If the overall community is better for it, even with all the risks in place, then ethically, a developer may be justified in its use.

Ethical Considerations

One concern people might have regarding Waldo vision is that the system creates a biometric profile that can be used to determine who a particular player is regardless of the game being played. So if you play a particular game for over a year, your profile could follow you to a new game you’ve just picked up. People may find concern in that a profile that is deemed to be cheating means that you may not just be banned from the game that you actually cheated in but you could also be banned in other games based off your profile. This may not take into account things like account recoveries. An account recovery is when a player gives someone else their account to play on. This is usually because the player offering up their account may not have a high enough skill for certain items or rewards in a game. Though this is usually frowned upon it’s categorized different from cheating; however, it’s very likely that the person performing the recovery could be using cheats in order to achieve the task at hand. In this scenario, we may be permanently banning the player who offered up the account while the player who actually used the cheats receives no punishment at all. This is obviously something that would need to be considered.
